<Section icon={Swords} title="Tactical Combat and Ballistic Systems">

The heart of the **urban strife demo** lies in its sophisticated combat engine. Unlike traditional turn-based games where every unit moves individually, Urban Strife employs a **Simultaneous Move AI** for the zombie swarms. This prevents the "waiting game" often found in the genre, making encounters with large groups feel fluid and dangerous. 

The ballistic system is equally impressive. Every bullet fired is treated as a physical object with a calculated trajectory. Factors such as weapon statistics and material density determine whether a shot will penetrate a wall or ricochet harmlessly. This adds a layer of depth where "cover" is relative; a wooden fence might hide you, but it won't stop a high-caliber rifle round.

| Action Type | AP Cost | Tactical Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Standard Shot** | Medium | Reliable damage based on weapon accuracy. |
| **Overwatch** | Variable | Automatically fire at enemies moving through your line of sight. |
| **Melee Strike** | Low | Silent takedowns; saves precious ammunition. |
| **Interact** | Low | Open doors, climb windows, or loot containers mid-combat. |
| **Reload** | Low/Medium | Essential maintenance; cost varies by weapon type. |

The management layer involves keeping group morale high while balancing the needs of your people. Workshops allow for the creation of weapon attachments and medical supplies, while gardens and barracks focus on the sustainability of food and manpower.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Current Limitations and Future Outlook">

As an Early Access title, the **urban strife demo** is a work in progress. While the core mechanics are robust, players should be aware of certain issues. The shift to AI-generated character portraits has been a point of contention among the community, as some feel they lack the charm of the original hand-drawn art. Additionally, the lack of a speed-up toggle for combat can make larger battles feel sluggish.

However, the developers at MicroProse have been active in responding to feedback. The demo is frequently updated to address bugs and refine the UI. For fans of classic turn-based strategy and deep survival RPGs, the innovation in the ballistic and AI systems makes Urban Strife a title to watch closely in 2026.

| Aspect | Current Status | Future Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |

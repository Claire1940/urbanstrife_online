<Section icon={Swords} title="Tactical Combat and Control Mastery">

Combat in Urban Strife is a deep, turn-based affair where every decision costs Action Points. Unlike standard RPGs, this game simulates bullet trajectories and material penetration. A wooden fence might provide visual concealment, but it won't stop a high-powered rifle round. Mastering the controls is the first step to becoming a legendary commander in the wasteland.

| Action / Mechanic | Control / Key | Tactical Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Swap Weapon** | **X** | Switch between primary and backup sidearms |
| **Fire Mode** | **F** | Toggle between single, burst, or auto fire |
| **Overwatch** | **Y** | Set a reaction zone for the enemy turn |
| **Group Select** | **0** | Move the entire squad simultaneously |
| **Crouch/Stealth** | **C** | Reduces detection and improves aim |
| **Targeting** | **Right Click** | Open the body-part targeting interface |

<Callout type="info" title="Tactical Tip">
Use **Overwatch** at chokepoints like doorways or narrow alleys. This forces zombies to walk into your line of fire, maximizing your AP efficiency during their turn.
</Callout>

### Combat Fundamentals
1.  **Initiative and Cover**: Always start combat from a crouched position behind solid cover. Characters in cover are less likely to be ambushed and gain significant defensive bonuses.
2.  **Noise Control**: Sound travels. Unsuppressed gunfire will attract every zombie in the vicinity. Use crossbows or melee weapons for isolated guards to maintain stealth.
3.  **Horde Defense**: When facing a large group of undead, retreat to a narrow entrance. Use shotguns and explosives to handle the front of the horde while your snipers pick off outliers.

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Accessing the Base: Step-by-Step Walkthrough">

Gaining entry to the National Guard Base requires a methodical approach to regional diplomacy and tactical exploration. You cannot simply blast your way through the front gates; instead, you must systematically build your reputation across the county.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish the Shelter Communication Network">
    Repair the city radio in the Urban Shelter to begin tracking transmissions from distant sectors. This allows you to monitor military movements and identify settlements that require immediate assistance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Resolve Independent Settlement Conflicts">
    Travel to regional sectors such as Little Italy and Old Port. Help independent camps, firefighters, and local barons survive roaming undead hordes to secure their political backing and future votes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Leverage Character Dialogue Checks">
    When interacting with faction representatives, use squad members with high Strength or Intelligence stats. Successful dialogue checks can resolve standoffs peacefully, preserving lives and earning massive reputation boosts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Secure the Required Votes">
    Present your accumulated regional support to the National Guard emissaries. Once the voting threshold is cleared, the coordinates to the mountain headquarters will be marked on your world map.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Saves Space Tip">
Before departing for the base, clear your squad's inventory. The military traders offer exceptionally heavy gear, and you will need maximum carrying capacity to haul your new arsenal back to the Shelter.
</Callout>

</Section>

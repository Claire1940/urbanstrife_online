<Section icon={Swords} title="Tactical Combat and Building Clearing">

Combat in **urban strife little italy** is defined by the game's realistic ballistics and material penetration systems. In this urban environment, the type of cover you choose determines whether you survive an encounter with human scavengers or the undead. A wooden door might stop a stray pellet from a shotgun, but it offers no protection against a hunting rifle. You must manage your **Action Points (AP)** carefully, ensuring that every survivor ends their turn behind substantial cover like brick walls or car engine blocks.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Breach and Clear">
    Approach the entrance while crouched to maintain stealth. Use a **Melee Fighter** or a survivor with a suppressed sidearm to open the door and check the immediate interior for "sleepers."
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Establish Overwatch">
    Once inside, position a **Ranged Shooter** near a window or doorway using the **Overwatch (Y)** command. This creates a kill zone for any enemies attracted by the noise of your entry.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Systematic Room Sweeps">
    Move the squad in a "buddy system" through the building. Never send a single survivor into an unmapped room. Use **Dexterity-based** survivors to interact with containers while others hold defensive angles.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Secure the Exit">
    Before looting, ensure all zombie spawners within the building are destroyed. This stops the infinite flow of undead and allows you to salvage materials without the pressure of an ongoing clock.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Stealth Mechanics and Environmental Factors">

Stealth in Urban Strife relies heavily on dynamic vision cones, represented by red squares on the tactical grid. When your squad members step into these red squares, enemy suspicion rises. If the suspicion meter fills completely, combat is initiated. To pull off a successful heist without firing a single shot, you must understand how environmental factors affect these vision cones.

Time of day plays a massive role in guard alertness. Attempting this heist during daylight hours is exceptionally difficult due to overlapping guard patrols and maximum visual range. However, waiting until after midnight alters the tactical landscape. Under the cover of darkness, guard vision ranges shrink, patrol routes become more predictable, and several guards settle into static, less observant positions.

| Environmental Factor | Day Conditions | Night Conditions (Post-Midnight) |
|---|---|---|
| Vision Cone Range | Maximum distance | Significantly reduced range |
| Guard Density | High active patrols | Static sentries, fewer active eyes |
| Detection Speed | Rapid detection | Slower suspicion accumulation |
| Safe Approach | Extremely difficult | Highly manageable with patience |

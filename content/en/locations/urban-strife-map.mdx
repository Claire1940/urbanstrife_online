<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat and Map Awareness">

Combat in Urban Strife is intrinsically linked to the **urban strife map** layout. The environment isn't just a backdrop; it’s a tactical tool. You must use the "Out" key to highlight interactable objects and potential cover. The game utilizes a turn-based Action Point (AP) system where every tile moved and every shot fired must be calculated against your remaining energy.

| Weapon Type | Optimal Range | AP Cost | Best Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Magnum .22** | Medium | 12-15 AP | Pick off lone scavengers |
| **Frying Pan/Melee** | Point Blank | 8-10 AP | Stealth kills or finishing downed foes |
| **Nail Gun** | Short | 10 AP | Inflicting status effects (Bleed) |
| **Crossbow** | Medium-Long | 18 AP | Silent takedowns from cover |

<Callout type="success" title="Tactical Advantage">

</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Scavenging Tactics and Resource Management">

Looting the **urban strife map** is a meticulous process. Unlike other RPGs, items in Urban Strife have significant weight and volume, meaning you cannot take everything. You must prioritize items based on your current shelter needs. If your generator is broken, focus on tools and wiring; if your team is starving, prioritize canned food and natural remedies.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure the Perimeter">
    Clear all zombies and cultists in the immediate vicinity before looting to avoid being ambushed while encumbered.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify High-Value Containers">
    Use the 'Out' key to scan for glowing objects. Focus on trunks, medicine cabinets, and toolboxes first.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Inventory Sorting">
    Distribute heavy items like car parts or garden shears among your strongest team members to avoid individual over-encumbrance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Extraction">
    Return to the map exit point once your inventory is full. Remember that traveling while overweight consumes significantly more stamina.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Heart} title="Rebuilding the Shelter Hospital">

Scavenging the **urban strife fema hospital** serves a larger strategic purpose: acquiring the materials, equipment, and blueprints required to upgrade your own Shelter Hospital. Rebuilding this vital facility is a multi-phase project that transitions your community from desperate survival to self-sufficiency.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure the Blueprints">
    Locate the specialized hospital renovation blueprints in the secure administrative offices of the FEMA facility.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Salvage Construction Materials">
    Gather steel pipes, wiring, and clean fixtures from the hospital wings to meet the initial Shelter building requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Recruit a Medical Specialist">
    Rescue or negotiate with a trained doctor or medic in Urban County to oversee your Shelter's medical operations.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Establish Clean Power">
    Connect your newly repaired hospital ward to a reliable Shelter power grid (solar, wind, or generator) to keep life-support systems online.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once operational, the Shelter Hospital provides massive passive benefits to your entire roster, ensuring that wounded militia members recover quickly between difficult expeditions.

| Upgrade Phase | Required Materials | Energy Cost | Passive Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Phase 1: Basic Ward** | Wood, scrap metal, basic bandages | None | Slow passive healing for idle survivors |
| **Phase 2: Operating Room** | Clean fixtures, surgical tools, wiring | Low | Treats severe trauma and deep bleeding |
| **Phase 3: ICU Ward** | Advanced electronics, chemicals, solar power | Medium | Maximizes recovery speed, minimizes scar penalties |

<Callout type="success" title="Upgrade Synergy">
Upgrading your Shelter Hospital alongside your Gardens or Hydroponics systems unlocks advanced crafting recipes, allowing you to produce high-tier medicine directly from home-grown herbs and salvaged chemicals.
</Callout>

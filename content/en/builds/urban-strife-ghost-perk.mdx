<Section icon={Shield} title="Survival Strategies for Ghost Users">

While the **urban strife ghost perk** makes you harder to hit, it doesn't make you invincible. If you are caught out of position, a stealth-focused character usually lacks the "zombie tank" resilience of a strength-build character. You must use the environment to your advantage.

- **Use Cover**: Even with high stealth, standing in the open is a recipe for disaster.
- **Manage Light**: Shoot out streetlights or use flares to distract enemies away from your path.
- **Diversionary Tactics**: Use Bubba or another loud character to draw attention to one side of the map while your Ghost infiltrates from the other.

<Callout type="tip" title="Advanced Tactic">

<Section icon={Shield} title="Bubba's Questline: Spoiled Sacramental Wine Mechanics">

The most common point of confusion in Bubba's questline revolves around the **Spoiled Cultist Sacramental Wine**. Many players clear out the nearby supermarket area, obtain regular cultist sacramental wine, and attempt to use it for the quest. However, regular wine cannot be turned into spoiled wine through any crafting recipe or wait mechanic.

The spoiled variant is an exclusive item tied directly to Sister Dawn's experiments in Adams Villa. If you have already angered the cultists or completed the arena scenarios in a way that makes the local NPCs hostile, obtaining this wine becomes significantly more complicated.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Sister Dawn">
    The primary cultist trader in Adams Villa who sells the Spoiled Sacramental Wine for 100g. Keeping her alive and friendly is the easiest route to success.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Father Joseph">
    The leader of the cultists. While the quest log may point to him, he does not directly trade or hand over the spoiled wine required for Bubba.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Corkscrew">
    The Biker faction representative who demands the spoiled wine. He will refuse to progress the quest if you only have regular sacramental wine.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

If Sister Dawn is killed during your playthrough, the game is designed to drop the Spoiled Sacramental Wine as loot from her body. However, relying on this method can be risky due to potential script errors, especially on older campaign saves. It is always safer to purchase the wine peacefully before initiating any hostile actions against the cultist faction.

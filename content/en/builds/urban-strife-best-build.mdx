<Section icon={Swords} title="Understanding Character Attributes">

In Urban Strife, your build's effectiveness is dictated by six core attributes. These attributes determine your success in combat, dialogue, and resource management. Rather than trying to be a "jack of all trades," focus your survivors on one primary role.

| Attribute | Primary Role Benefit | Key Interaction |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Marksmanship** | Increases ranged accuracy | Affects all firearm and crossbow hits |
| **Strength** | Boosts carry capacity and melee damage | Enables intimidation dialogue options |
| **Agility** | Improves movement AP and interrupt chance | Crucial for stealth and melee evasion |
| **Dexterity** | Lowers item use AP costs | Vital for medics and utility specialists |
| **Intelligence** | Accelerates attribute growth | Unlocks unique quest solutions |
| **Endurance** | Increases max HP and stamina | Essential for all frontline survivors |

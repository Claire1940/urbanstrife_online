As you upgrade your shelter, you unlock global **urban strife perks** that provide bonuses to all team members. <Section icon={Sparkles} title="Advanced Tactical Tips">

To truly master the **urban strife perks** system, you must look beyond basic stats and utilize the game's deeper mechanics. Flanking, line-of-sight manipulation, and limb-specific injuries play a massive role in high-level play.

- **Limb Targeting**: If an enemy has a dangerous weapon, target their arms to reduce their accuracy. To stop a runner, target the legs.
- **Overwatch Stacking**: Place multiple characters on overwatch covering a single choke point to create a "kill zone" that can wipe out entire scavenger squads.
- **Crafting Efficiency**: Use Billy Bow for crafting melee weapons and Lucy Lee for medical supplies to benefit from their natural proficiency bonuses.
- **Inventory Swapping**: You can swap weapons between characters during combat if they are adjacent, allowing you to adapt to changing ranges instantly.

| Tactical Action | AP Cost | Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Crouch** | 2 AP | +Accuracy, -Profile |
| **Run** | Variable | High mobility, High noise |
| **Sneak** | Variable | Low noise, Stealth kills |
| **Reload** | 4-6 AP | Essential for firearms |
| **Swap Weapon** | 3 AP | Adapt to range |

<Callout type="tip" title="Final Advice">
Don't get attached to a single weapon. If you kill an enemy with a better gun, pick it up and use it immediately. In Urban Strife, the best gear is often whatever you just took from your opponent.
</Callout>

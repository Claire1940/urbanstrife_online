<Section icon={Target} title="High-Power Firearms and Penetration">

When dealing with the Rogue Army or well-equipped Biker gangs, you need raw stopping power. Urban Strife features a realistic ballistic system where bullet attenuation and material thickness determine if a shot reaches its target. High-powered rifles like the **Hunting Rifle** or the rare **.50 BMG Anti-Materiel Rifle** can fire through walls that would stop a 9mm round cold.

The **urban strife best weapons** for long-range engagements are those with high penetration stats. If an enemy is hiding behind a car door, a standard pistol shot will likely be absorbed. However, a sniper rifle shot will penetrate the door and hit the target, albeit with slightly reduced damage.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Weapon Maintenance and Owen's Repairs">

Even the **urban strife best weapons** will eventually fail if not maintained. Version 1.0 introduced a robust repair system that prevents your favorite gear from becoming scrap. Weapons and body armor lose durability with every use or hit taken. Once durability hits zero, the item becomes "Broken" and loses most of its combat effectiveness.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Salvage Surplus Gear">
    Don't sell every duplicate pistol you find. Salvage unwanted weapons at the Shelter to produce "Weapon Parts." These are the primary currency for repairs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlock Owen's Operation">
    Progress through the early campaign until Owen's repair services become available at the Shelter Workshop. You cannot perform high-tier repairs in the field.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Apply Tactical Kits">
    Use the Gunsmith Workbench to attach scopes, silencers, and high-capacity magazines. These modifications improve the weapon's base stats without increasing AP costs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Monitor Ballistic Plates">
    Remember that while body armor can be repaired, the **Ballistic Plates** inside are expendable. Once they shatter, you must craft or find new ones.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Attachment Type | Compatibility | Primary Effect | Crafting Material |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Silencer** | Pistols/SMGs/Rifles | Reduces noise radius by 70% | Chemicals, Metal |
| **Optical Scope** | Rifles/Snipers | Increases hit chance at long range | Glass, Components |
| **Extended Mag** | Automatics/Pistols | Reduces reload frequency | Metal, Springs |
| **Laser Sight** | Pistols/SMGs | Improves hip-fire accuracy | Electronics, Components |

<Callout type="info" title="Repair Priority">
Always prioritize repairing your primary Sniper Rifle and Medic's sidearm. A broken rifle in the middle of a human firefight is a death sentence for your squad.
</Callout>

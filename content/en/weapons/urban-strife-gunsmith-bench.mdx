<Section icon={Wrench} title="The Role of the urban strife gunsmith bench in Version 1.0">

In the 1.0 release of Urban Strife, the **urban strife gunsmith bench** serves as the backbone of your militia's offensive capabilities. Unlike the early access versions, the full release introduces a comprehensive durability system where weapons and body armor degrade through use and combat damage. The gunsmith bench and the associated workshop facilities are no longer optional upgrades; they are survival necessities that determine whether your squad can survive prolonged expeditions into Urban County.

The workshop environment is divided into several specialized stations. While basic repairs can be handled once Owen's operation is active, the gunsmith bench specifically focuses on high-precision modifications. This includes the fabrication of silencers for stealth operations, the installation of high-capacity magazines for horde control, and the application of tactical weapon kits that improve accuracy and Action Point efficiency.

<Callout type="tip" title="Efficiency Tip">
Always salvage duplicate or low-quality weapons before they reach 0% durability. Salvaging items with some remaining condition yields more high-quality components for the gunsmith bench.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Settings} title="Unlocking the Workshop and Owen's Repair Services">

Accessing the **urban strife gunsmith bench** requires progressing through the initial Shelter restoration phases. You cannot simply build these facilities from the start; you must rescue specific specialists and clear the necessary sectors to secure the tools required for advanced gunsmithing.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Complete the Prologue">
    Follow the opening story where the CDC patient is brought to Urban Shelter. This phase introduces the basic inventory and equipment management systems.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Repair the Shelter Workshop">
    The workshop is one of the five essential services (alongside the radio, hospital, auto shop, and barracks). You must allocate construction materials to restore this facility.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initiate Owen's Operation">
    Once the workshop is functional, Owen will offer repair services. This unlocks the ability to use salvaged parts to fix damaged firearms and body armor.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Secure Blueprints and Tools">
    Advanced modifications at the gunsmith bench require specific blueprints found during exploration or earned through faction alliances like the Shady Lady Bikers.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Limitation">
While the gunsmith bench can repair the housing of body armor, ballistic plates are considered expendable. Once a plate is destroyed, it must be replaced entirely rather than repaired.
</Callout>

</Section>

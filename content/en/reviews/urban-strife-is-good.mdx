<Section icon={Swords} title="Tactical Combat and AP Management">

Combat in Urban Strife is built entirely around Action Points (AP), dynamic sightlines, and noise generation. To survive the dangerous sectors of Urban County, you must understand how to balance aggressive maneuvers with defensive positioning.

**Video Highlights:**
- **Dynamic AP Planning**: Previewing movement paths to preserve AP for attacks.
- **Overwatch Positioning**: Creating overlapping firing lanes in narrow choke points.
- **Noise Control**: Utilizing silent weapons to clear maps without alerting the horde.

<Callout type="warning" title="Sound Matters">
Every firearm blast emits a specific noise radius. Unsuppressed shotgun blasts or grenade explosions will pull nearby zombies from adjacent streets directly into your active combat zone.
</Callout>

### Human Opponents vs. Zombie Hordes

Fighting living humans is a game of angles, flanking, and cover penetration. Human enemies utilize defensive positions, throw explosives, and coordinate their fire. Conversely, fighting undead hordes requires managing choke points and using wide-spread crowd control weapons during their dedicated simultaneous movement phase.

| Combat Scenario | Ideal Weapon Class | Primary Tactical Goal | Recommended Cover |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Isolated Zombies** | Blades, Crossbows, Blunt | Silent elimination, save ammo | Light cover / Stealth |
| **Zombie Hordes** | Shotguns, Molotovs, SMGs | Choke point control, area denial | Elevated platforms |
| **Human Snipers** | Hunting Rifles, Snipers | Long-range flanking, headshots | Heavy brick/concrete |
| **Armored Soldiers** | High-caliber Rifles, Grenades | Cover destruction, AP drain | Engine blocks |

Rebuilding your central base, the Urban Shelter, is crucial to surviving the final endgame siege. The Shelter is not just a storage dump; it is a living community that requires food, water, power, and high morale to function.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Stabilize Daily Needs">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Restore Town Services">

  <Step num="3" title="Establish Food Industries">

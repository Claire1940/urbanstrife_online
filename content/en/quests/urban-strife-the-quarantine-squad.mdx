<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Walkthrough">

Navigating the port requires a methodical approach. The zombies in this area are sensitive to noise, meaning a single loud gunshot can pull enemies from both walled sections simultaneously. Follow these steps to complete **urban strife the quarantine squad** efficiently.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure the Perimeter">
    Approach the first walled area where Ford is located. Instead of rushing in, use a suppressed weapon to pick off the stragglers near the gate. Once the immediate area is clear, move your squad inside but keep one member near the exit as a fallback point.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Clear the Undead Waves">
    The body piles act as spawn points. As you approach them, waves of zombies will emerge. Focus on clearing these spawns before attempting to burn the piles. Killing the zombies first ensures your "thrower" isn't interrupted during the animation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Incinerate the Body Piles">
    Locate the four piles of corpses. Equipping a Molotov, target the center of the pile. You will see a prompt or a visual change in the pile once the fire takes hold. Ensure all four are burning simultaneously to trigger the quest update.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Final Area Sweep">
    After the piles are destroyed, a final wave often spawns near the second port area. Check every corner of the walled sections, as a single remaining zombie can prevent the quest from transitioning to the "Talk to Quest Giver" phase.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Timing">
Some players report that burning the piles *too early*—specifically before all scripted spawns have appeared—can break the quest logic. It is safer to kill all visible zombies in the immediate vicinity before throwing your first Molotov.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={AlertTriangle} title="Troubleshooting the Quarantine Squad Bug">

Many survivors have encountered a frustrating issue where the quest fails to complete even after all zombies are dead and piles are burned. This is often related to a script flag failing to trigger in the **urban strife the quarantine squad** mission. If your quest log still says "Burn the body piles" despite them being ash, follow these troubleshooting steps.

| Issue | Potential Cause | 2026 Fix/Workaround |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Quest won't update** | Scripted zombie stuck in walls | Use a grenade near containers to flush them out |
| **Piles won't burn** | Wrong damage type used | Ensure you use **Fire** damage, not Explosive |
| **Ford won't talk** | Combat state is still active | Retreat to the world map and re-enter the zone |
| **NPC Missing** | Quest sequence break | Reload the autosave created upon entering the Port |

<Callout type="info" title="Developer Insight">
The developers have confirmed that killing zombies for EXP *before* burning the piles is the intended way to play, but doing so too slowly can sometimes cause the script to hang. If the quest persists in its bugged state, sending your save file to the support team is the only way to fix a corrupted run.
</Callout>

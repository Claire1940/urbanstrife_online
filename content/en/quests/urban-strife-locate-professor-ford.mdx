<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Tactics and Weapon Selection">

While trying to **urban strife locate professor ford**, you will inevitably face combat. The current balance in Urban Strife favors melee weapons due to their lack of noise and high stagger potential. However, a balanced loadout is necessary for dealing with armored scavengers.

**Recommended Weapon Loadout for Old Port:**
- **Primary**: Hunting Bow (Silent, reusable ammo).
- **Secondary**: Machete or Spiked Bat (High stagger, no ammo cost).
- **Utility**: Nail Bombs (For emergency crowd control).

| Weapon Category | Pros | Cons |

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Aligning with The Firm">

To successfully secure an alliance with the corporate mercenaries, players must follow a specific progression path. This involves balancing early-game survival with deliberate diplomatic actions that build trust with corporate representatives.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish Initial Contact">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Secure Key Blueprints">
    Trade high-value scavenged electronics to unlock the initial tier of military-grade crafting blueprints at your shelter.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Sabotage Rival Operations">
    Undertake covert missions to disrupt the supply lines of the bikers and cultists, forcing them to reconsider their presence in the area.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Finalize the Alliance">
    Commit to the final faction vote in the shelter, officially declaring your partnership and preparing for the incoming zombie horde.
  </Step>
</Steps>

By following these steps, you ensure that your shelter is backed by the strongest military force in the region when the final siege begins.

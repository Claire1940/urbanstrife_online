<Section icon={Map} title="Understanding the Urban Strife: The Feds Quest">

In the post-apocalyptic landscape of Urban County, **Urban Strife: The Feds Quest** serves as a bridge between local survival and the larger political struggle for the region. The "Feds" primarily refers to the remnants of the National Guard, established in a heavily fortified mountain base. This quest line is not merely a combat challenge; it is a test of your Shelter's legitimacy and your ability to navigate the complex loyalties of the Rogue Army Garrison.

| Quest Phase | Primary Location | Main Objective | Difficulty |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Initial Contact** | Urban County Outskirts | Locate federal scouts and establish a dialogue link | Tier 1 |
| **Verification** | Mountain Base Gates | Prove the Shelter's value through resource donation or service | Tier 2 |
| **The Deep Dive** | Underground Facility | Investigate federal motives or secure the alliance | Tier 3 |
| **Final Extraction** | Faction HQ | Escape with blueprints or finalize the military treaty | Tier 3+ |

<Callout type="tip" title="Reputation Matters">
Before initiating this quest, ensure your squad includes a survivor with high **Intelligence** or a military background. This can unlock dialogue options that bypass expensive combat encounters or resource-heavy bribes.
</Callout>

Building reputation with the Feds allows you to access the most advanced trading inventory in the game. Unlike the Shady Lady Bikers or the Church of Second Chance, the National Guard focuses on standardized, high-penetration equipment that is essential for dealing with armored undead in the late game.

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Walkthrough for The Feds Quest">

Navigating the federal questline requires a balance of tactical positioning and social engineering. Follow these steps to ensure you don't alienate the military power before securing their advanced technology.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure the Radio Link">
    Before the quest triggers, you must repair the **City Radio** at the Urban Shelter. Once operational, listen for encrypted federal broadcasts. You will need to travel to a designated sector in the northern hills to meet a contact. Avoid bringing the full militia to the first meeting to keep tension low.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Resource Verification">
    The Feds will demand proof that your Shelter is not a drain on regional resources. You must provide a specific amount of **Medicine** and **Fuel**. Alternatively, if your **Strength** check is high enough, you can intimidate the scouts into granting access based on your combat record.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Infiltrating the Mountain Base">
    Once at the gates, you enter the **Alliance Phase**. You must navigate the base without drawing weapons. Use this time to identify the location of the **Army HMMWV** and the primary quartermaster. Your goal is to meet the Commanding Officer to discuss the **Atlanta Horde** threat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Internal Conflict">
    You will discover a rift within the Garrison. You must choose to support the established leadership or a splinter group of soldiers who want to share supplies with the civilian population. This choice determines your long-term **Faction Rewards**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Combat Warning">
If you choose the hostile route during the infiltration phase, be prepared for high-accuracy sniper fire from the base ramparts. The Feds use **Overwatch** more effectively than any other faction in the game.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Best Loadouts for Federal Missions">

Missions involving the Feds often pit you against well-armored human opponents or dense clusters of soldiers. Standard melee and low-caliber pistols are often insufficient. You need weapons with high **Material Penetration** and armor-piercing capabilities.

| Weapon Type | Recommended Model | Tactical Role | AP Efficiency |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Assault Rifle** | M16 / AK-74 | Mid-range suppression and burst fire | High |
| **Sniper Rifle** | Hunting Rifle (Scoped) | Priority target removal (Officers/Snipers) | Moderate |
| **Shotgun** | Pump Action | Close-quarters defense against breach teams | Moderate |
| **Explosives** | Frag Grenades | Clearing entrenched positions behind cover | Low |

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Armor Piercing Priority">
    The Feds wear high-tier **Body Armor** and **Ballistic Plates**. Use 5.56mm or 7.62mm ammunition. Hollow-point rounds are ineffective here; stick to standard or armor-piercing variants to ensure damage reaches the target.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Stealth Approach">
    If your **Agility** is high, use suppressed pistols to thin the outer guard. However, once the alarm sounds, the military will use flares to negate your vision advantage, making traditional stealth much harder.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="success" title="Weapon Tip">
The **.50 BMG Anti-Materiel Rifle**, while rare, can penetrate the thickest walls of the mountain base. If you find one before this quest, it can trivialise the boss encounter by allowing you to fire from outside the detection range.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Faction Comparison: Feds vs. Bikers vs. Cult">

Choosing to align with the Feds during this quest has significant consequences for your Shelter's endgame. The alliance you secure determines the specific **Powered Upgrades** available for the final defense on Day 20.

| Feature | National Guard (Feds) | Shady Lady Bikers | Church of Second Chance |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary Reward** | Military Hardware & Vehicles | Black Market Gear & Repairs | Unique Materials & Recruits |
| **Defense Bonus** | Automatic Turrets | Reinforced Barricades | Morale & Stamina Buffs |
| **Siege Support** | Air Support / Snipers | Mobile Strike Teams | Undead Decoys |
| **Recruit Type** | Disciplined Soldiers | Skilled Mechanics | Spiritual Healers |

<Callout type="info" title="Long-term Impact">
An alliance with the Feds usually makes the **Shady Lady Bikers** hostile. This means you will lose access to the biker bar's trade network but gain the military's logistical support, which is superior for the final **Atlanta Horde** siege.
</Callout>

</Section>

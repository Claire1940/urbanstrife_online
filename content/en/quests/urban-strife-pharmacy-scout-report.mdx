<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step: Obtaining the Scout Report">

Securing the **urban strife pharmacy scout report** requires a methodical approach. The pharmacy is often surrounded by a "soft" perimeter of zombies that can easily turn into a "hard" encounter if you use unsuppressed firearms. Following this route ensures you minimize resource consumption while maximizing your tactical advantage.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Perimeter Scouting">
    Approach the pharmacy from the rear alleyway. Use a character with high **Agility** to scout the windows. Avoid the front entrance, as it is often blocked by debris and attracts more attention from street-roaming hordes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Silent Entry">
    Use a crowbar or lockpicks on the back door. If your **Dexterity** is high enough, lockpicking is preferred to keep the noise meter at zero. Once inside, immediately crouch to reduce your detection profile.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Clear the Interior">
    The pharmacy usually contains 3-4 zombies trapped behind the counters. Use **Interrupt Fire** with crossbows or execute silent melee takedowns. Do not use explosives inside, as they may destroy the containers holding the report.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Retrieve the Intel">
    The report is typically located in the manager's office in the back or inside a locked floor safe behind the prescription counter. Search all "Quest Item" highlighted containers first.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Exfiltration">
    Once the report is in your inventory, exit through the same back door. Do not linger to loot every shelf if the "Horde Awareness" meter is rising. Return to the World Map to trigger the faction delivery dialogue.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
In the 1.0 version, certain sectors have "Zombie Spawners" located in nearby basements. If you take too long to retrieve the report, the pharmacy may become overrun by a secondary wave of undead.
</Callout>

</Section>

Once you have the **urban strife pharmacy scout report**, you must decide which faction to support. This decision has long-term consequences for your 2026 campaign progression. ### Faction Impact Analysis
- **The Bikers**: Handing the report to the Shady Lady charter improves your standing with the "profit-driven" side of Urban County. They will unlock the **Gunsmith Workbench** upgrades earlier, allowing you to repair damaged firearms found in the field.
- **The Militia**: Choosing the National Guard remnants or local Militia focuses on community stability. They provide blueprints for the **Hospital** upgrade in your Shelter, which is essential for treating "Infection" and "Deep Wounds" sustained during later horde sieges.

<Callout type="info" title="Reputation Update">
Reputation in Urban Strife is a zero-sum game early on. Gaining favor with the Bikers will slightly decrease your standing with the Militia, which may lead to higher "toll" costs when traveling through their controlled sectors.
</Callout>

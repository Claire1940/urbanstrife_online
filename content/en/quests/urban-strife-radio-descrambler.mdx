<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Radio Fix Procedure">

Restoring the radio isn't just about finding the **urban strife radio descrambler**; it's a multi-stage process that involves stealth, combat, and resource management. Follow these steps to ensure you don't get trapped by the local scavenger hordes while attempting the repair.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Infiltrate the Old Port">
    Approach the Old Port from the southern entrance. Use the "Eyes Peeled" mechanic to spot patrolling scavengers. It is highly recommended to enter during night hours to maximize your stealth bonuses, especially if your character has high Dexterity.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Secure the Harbor Master Office">
    The office is usually locked. You can pick the lock if you have the necessary skills or search the nearby "Dead Civilian" for a key. Once inside, prioritize clearing the room silently. Melee weapons like the Saber or a modified bat are incredibly effective here.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locate the Descrambler Unit">
    The descrambler is located in a specialized container labeled "Army Quarantine Goods." Interacting with this will trigger a dialogue or a skill check depending on your current party composition.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Return to the Shelter">
    Once the descrambler is in your inventory, avoid the main roads. Use the alternate exits to bypass the "Kisses" scavenger group. Returning to the shelter and speaking with the technical lead will complete the "Radio Fix" objective.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Enemy Alert">
The "Kisses" scavenger group often patrols the perimeter of the Harbor Master office. If spotted, they will call for reinforcements, potentially surrounding your squad in the narrow hallways.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Mastering Turn-Based Combat Tactics">

Combat in Urban Strife revolves around a granular Action Point (AP) system. Unlike simpler TRPGs, you have total control over how you distribute your points between movement, aiming, and utilizing different firing modes. The game also features a specialized "Swarm AI" designed to speed up the turns of large groups of weaker enemies, though player turns remain deliberate and tactical.

| Action Type | AP Cost (Average) | Tactical Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Snap Shot** | 3-4 AP | Quick, low accuracy |
| **Aimed Shot** | 6-8 AP | **High crit chance** |
| **Crouch Stance** | 2 AP | Improved cover bonus |
| **Prone Stance** | 3 AP | **Max accuracy / Stealth** |
| **Reloading** | 4-5 AP | Essential maintenance |

**Key Combat Features:**
- **Limb Targeting**: You can target specific body parts to disable enemies. Targeting legs reduces movement, while headshots offer a chance for instant kills.
- **Stance System**: Your stance (Running, Crouched, Stealth) determines your visibility to enemies and the effectiveness of the cover you occupy.
- **Ballistics**: Different weapons have varying ranges and armor penetration values, making loadout choice vital before entering a zone.

<Callout type="tip" title="Tactical Advantage">
Use the "Wait" command to let enemies exhaust their AP while moving toward you. This allows your mercenaries to spend their full AP bar on high-accuracy shots once the enemies are out in the open.
</Callout>

</Section>

Before the bullets fly, you explore maps in real-time. This phase is crucial for gathering resources and positioning your squad. Enemies have distinct sight and hearing ranges; if you stay out of their vision cones and move quietly, you can initiate combat on your own terms.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Scout the Perimeter">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Set the Ambush">

  <Step num="3" title="Resource Vacuuming">

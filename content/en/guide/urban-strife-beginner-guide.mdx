<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics and Tactical Aiming">

Combat in Urban Strife is turn-based and highly tactical. The aiming system relies on line of sight, angles, and exposed body parts. You can only target body parts that are actively exposed from behind cover. While headshots deal devastating damage, they are difficult to land early in the game without proper skills.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Assess Enemy Exposure">
    Check which body parts of the target are exposed. If an enemy is behind high cover, headshots might be completely blocked.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Choose the Right Weapon">
    Use melee or blunt weapons on isolated targets to save precious ammo. Save shotguns and rifles for armored threats.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Manage Action Points (AP)">
    Always leave enough AP at the end of your turn to seek cover or reload your weapon. Standing in the open is a death sentence.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Aiming Mechanics">
If you find your hit chances are low, try repositioning to get a better angle around the enemy's cover. Even a slight step to the side can reveal fully exposed limbs.
</Callout>

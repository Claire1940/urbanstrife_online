<Section icon={Swords} title="Tactical Combat and Action Point Mastery">

Combat in this tactical RPG relies on simulated bullet trajectories, material penetration, and precise Action Point (AP) management. Whether you are fighting rival human factions or defending against a massive wave of zombies, positioning and noise control are the keys to victory.

### Combat Mechanics Comparison

| Tactical Element | Combat Application | Key Limitations |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Action Points (AP)** | Dictates movement, attacks, and item usage | Running out of AP in the open leaves characters vulnerable |
| **Stealth & Noise** | Uses melee and crossbows to prevent alerting groups | Fails instantly if a loud firearm is discharged |
| **Overwatch (Y)** | Sets up reaction attacks during the enemy turn | Firing lanes can be blocked by moving teammates |
| **Body-Part Targeting** | Selects heads for damage or limbs to cripple targets | Headshots have significantly lower base hit chances |

  <Card title="Zombie Horde Defense">

<Section icon={Target} title="Survivor Development and Specialist Builds">

Your squad members grow stronger by performing actions in the field. Repeatedly firing weapons, sneaking, or treating wounds directly improves the corresponding attributes. To build an efficient team, assign specific roles to your survivors and let their field experience reinforce their strengths.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify Native Perks">
    Check each recruit's biography, native perks, and unique defining traits. Two survivors might fill the same role but excel in different situations based on their backgrounds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Train Attributes through Actions">
    Have your designated shooter make ranged attacks, let your medic apply all healing items, and keep your stealth scout crouching to naturally grow their core attributes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Select Matching Profession Perks">
    Use the three-tier profession perk system to reinforce your survivor's primary job. Avoid forcing a utility specialist with high crafting skills into a pure frontline combat role.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Specialist Role | Core Attributes | Primary Field Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ranged Shooter** | Marksmanship, Agility | Securing Overwatch lanes and removing high-value targets |
| **Melee Fighter** | Strength, Dexterity, Endurance | Conserving ammunition and executing silent takedowns |
| **Stealth Scout** | Agility, Dexterity | Spotting enemy positions and setting up quiet ambushes |
| **Combat Medic** | Dexterity, Intelligence, Endurance | Keeping injured fighters operational under heavy pressure |

<Callout type="info" title="Note">
Intelligence is a highly valuable attribute because it increases the overall progression speed of all other attributes and unlocks unique dialogue options.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Shelter Upgrades and Crafting Priorities">

The Shelter serves as your operational base and the ultimate test of your survival strategy. Upgrading your facilities in a logical order ensures your community remains fed, healthy, and well-defended before the final campaign siege.

### Shelter Upgrade Path

| Phase | Priority | Facility Focus | Strategic Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. Stabilize** | Immediate | Food, water, fuel, and power | Prevents daily resource decay and keeps facilities running |
| **2. Restore** | High | Radio, hospital, workshop, barracks | Unlocks core campaign progression and squad healing |
| **3. Produce** | High | Gardens, hydroponics, rat farms | Reduces reliance on scavenging trips for basic food |
| **4. Armory** | Medium | Retooled workshop, gunsmith bench | Enables ammo crafting, weapon repairs, and attachments |
| **5. Defense** | Late Game | Fortifications, powered fences, solar | Prepares the physical base for the Day 20 horde assault |

<Callout type="success" title="Success">
You can craft items directly using resources stored in your base stash or nearby loot containers, which eliminates the need to manually shuffle items in your inventory.
</Callout>

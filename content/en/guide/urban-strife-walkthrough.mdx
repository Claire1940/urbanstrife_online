<Section icon={Target} title="Little Italy Walkthrough: Dealing with Moonshiners">

Once you clear the initial bandit threat around Adams Villa, your search for the missing Professor Ford leads you to Little Italy. Here, you will encounter the local merchant Honest Joe and his crew.

### Step-by-Step Quest Progression

1. **Locate Honest Joe's Emporium**: Head to the local barber shop in Little Italy to find Honest Joe and his guard Bubba.
2. **Accept Bubba's Request**: Speak with Bubba to learn about a group of hostile, rowdy Moonshiners squatting in a nearby house.
3. **Flank the Squatters**: Position your long-range characters along the exterior fence line while your shotgun-wielding units breach the front door.
4. **Clear the Bathroom Zombie**: Watch out for an isolated zombie trapped inside the squatters' bathroom during your post-fight sweep.
5. **Unlock Faction Merchants**: Return to Bubba to unlock specialized merchants: Slimy Ned (mechanic), Bangs (demolitions), and Corkscrew (liquor).

<Callout type="success" title="Quest Reward">
Completing Bubba's task unlocks access to high-tier trade goods, including 12-Gauge tactical shotguns and valuable crafting materials.
</Callout>

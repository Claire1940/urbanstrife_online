<Section icon={Shield} title="Equipment Repairs and Salvaging Rules">

In the Version 1.0 release, weapons and body armor decay dynamically as they suffer wear-and-tear or absorb ballistic impacts. Operating damaged firearms increases the risk of malfunctions, while degraded armor offers significantly reduced protection against incoming rounds.

To maintain your arsenal, you must establish a steady recycling loop. Salvaging unwanted weapons and armor produces specialized repair parts. By default, the game excludes currently equipped gear from the salvage screen to prevent accidental destruction of your active loadouts.

| Equipment Type | Degradation Trigger | Repair Method | Special Rules |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Firearms** | Firing rounds, environmental exposure | Owen's Repair Bench using weapon parts | High-tier weapons require more advanced parts |
| **Body Armor** | Absorbing ballistic or melee hits | Owen's Repair Bench using armor parts | Fabric backing can be fully restored |
| **Ballistic Plates** | Directly absorbing high-penetration shots | Cannot be repaired | Must be replaced with fresh plates |
| **Melee Tools** | Striking physical targets or doors | Standard workshop repair | Durability decays rapidly on hard surfaces |

<Callout type="warning" title="Expendable Armor Plates">
Do not waste resources trying to repair ballistic plates. These are designed as disposable inserts. Keep a stockpile of replacement plates in your stash and swap them out before embarking on high-difficulty sector missions.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Step-by-Step Workshop Progression">

To fully optimize your crafting capabilities, you must upgrade your workshop in structured phases. Trying to craft high-tier military ammunition before securing basic tool access will stall your economy. Follow this progression path to establish an efficient industrial pipeline.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure Basic Construction Materials">
    Scavenge nearby suburban sectors and residential blocks to gather wood, scrap metal, and hardware components. Keep these in your global stash rather than selling them to early traders.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Restore the Town-Center Workshop">
    Spend your initial construction materials to repair the physical workshop building in the Shelter town center. This unlocks basic crafting options like Molotov cocktails and simple melee reinforcements.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock Owen's Repair Operation">
    Complete the recruitment and localized quests for Owen. Once he is stationed at your workshop, the repair interface becomes active, allowing you to restore damaged firearms.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Upgrade to Level 2 (Industrial Press)">
    Invest rare electronic components and machinery parts to upgrade the workshop. This unlocks the Ammo Press, enabling the fabrication of custom ammunition types such as Dum-Dum and explosive rounds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Integrate Faction Blueprints">
    Establish alliances with major factions like the National Guard, the Church of Second Chance, or the Shady Lady Bikers. High-level reputation unlocks exclusive blueprints for advanced tactical gear.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Blueprint Acquisition">
While basic blueprints are unlocked automatically by upgrading the workshop, rare and specialized recipes are hidden throughout Urban County. Check with faction traders and search secure safes in military checkpoints to find them.
</Callout>

</Section>

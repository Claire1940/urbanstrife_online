<Section icon={Shield} title="Understanding Infection Mechanics">

Managing the health of your survivors is the highest priority in Urban Strife. When a team member becomes infected, their survival depends on your ability to secure consistent medical intervention. Currently, the infection cannot be "cured" instantly with a single item; instead, it relies on a recurring maintenance cycle at your base.

**Essential Survival Data:**

| Status | Effect | Requirement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Infected | Time-limited health | 24h base visit |
| Healthy | Full combat capability | Regular maintenance |
| Reserve | Paused infection progression | Swap at base |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">
If you notice an infection timer running low on a critical character, immediately swap them into your reserve roster. This prevents them from turning while you focus on other mission objectives.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Managing Infected Team Members">

To keep your team alive, you must understand the interaction between the infection and your base facilities. Dr. Henderson serves as the primary medical authority for managing these conditions.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Monitor Health Status">
    Regularly check your team's vitals after combat encounters to identify early signs of infection.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Return to Base">
    Visit the shelter every 24 hours to trigger the automatic antiviral administration process.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Validate Recovery">
    Ensure the 24-hour clock updates correctly; if no interaction occurs, verify the character's injury status.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Utilize Reserves">
    If the treatment fails or you need to prioritize other tasks, move the infected member to the reserve list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Developer Note">
The development team has acknowledged that some players may encounter bugs where the 24-hour clock fails to reset. If your character is not receiving treatment, ensure you are not saving and swapping immediately, as this can cause roster errors.
</Callout>

</Section>

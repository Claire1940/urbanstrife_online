In the brutal world of this tactical RPG, **urban strife electronics** represent the bridge between primitive survival and modern tactical dominance. <Section icon={Wrench} title="Dismantling and Extraction Process">

You don't always find **urban strife electronics** in a ready-to-use state. Most of your high-tier components will come from dismantling complex items found in the world. This process requires specific tools and a character with a decent "Technical" or "Crafting" skill level to ensure you don't damage the delicate parts during extraction.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire the Right Tools">
    Ensure your scavenger is equipped with a **Multitool** or a **Screwdriver**. Without these, you will only be able to smash items for basic scrap, losing out on rare circuit boards.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify High-Value Targets">
    Prioritize items like desktop computers, television sets, and microwave ovens. These contain the densest concentration of electronic parts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Perform the Extraction">
    Use the 'Dismantle' action from the inventory or interaction menu. Your character's skill level will determine the percentage chance of receiving "Advanced" components vs "Basic" scrap.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Sort and Store">
    Convert bulky items into stackable components immediately. This allows you to stay in the field longer and collect more **urban strife electronics** before returning to your shelter.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Item to Dismantle | Guaranteed Loot | Potential Bonus Loot | Skill Req. |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Desktop PC** | 2x Circuit Board | 1x Cooling Fan, 3x Wire | Medium |
| **Radio** | 1x Scrap Electronics | 1x Copper Wire | Low |
| **Industrial Panel** | 3x Heavy Wire | 1x Power Cell | High |
| **Security Camera** | 1x Sensor | 1x Optical Lens | High |

<Callout type="success" title="Efficiency Bonus">

</Callout>

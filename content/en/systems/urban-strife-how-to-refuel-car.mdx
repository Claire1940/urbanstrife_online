Refueling your vehicle requires a mix of base preparation and field interaction. You cannot simply jump into an abandoned car and drive away. You must first secure the fuel, transport it to your vehicle, and use the correct interface commands to fill the tank.

Follow these precise steps to get your vehicle running and fully fueled for long-distance expeditions:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Scavenge Fuel Canisters">
    Locate fuel canisters or siphoning tools in industrial zones, gas stations, or abandoned cars. Keep these canisters in your squad's inventory or active quick slots.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Approach the Target Vehicle">
    Bring your squad members close to the vehicle you wish to fuel. Ensure the immediate area is clear of zombies or hostile faction scouts to prevent interruptions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Open the Vehicle Interface">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Transfer and Apply Fuel">
    Drag the fuel canister from your character's inventory into the vehicle's fuel slot. Confirm the action to empty the canister and increase the vehicle's fuel percentage.
  </Step>
